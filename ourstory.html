Pengar-fund’s history

The concept behind Pengar’s internal fund tracking was born in 2023 with a clear mission: to deliver structured and actionable insights into revenue growth, strategic investments, and long-term sustainable reinvestment planning. By creating a framework for transparent financial decision-making, Pengar empowers teams across the company to align their goals, manage resources effectively, and pursue innovation with confidence. This internal system is designed not only to monitor financial performance but also to drive intelligent growth initiatives, ensuring that Pengar’s success is sustainable and forward-looking.

2023

Business launch. Initial capital investment of 500,000 SEK.

2024

Monthly revenue surpassed 100,000 SEK. Internal fund established for team growth and R&D.

2025

The fund value exceeds 2,000,000 SEK. Expansion plan initiated across EU markets.

2026 (planned)

Targeting 5,000,000 SEK in internal fund value. New fintech product release expected.
